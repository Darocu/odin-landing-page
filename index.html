Welcome to the Dragon Crest Expanse!

The world began with the Age of Dragons. All that existed were mythical creatures of grandesque power. They slowly grew bored of each other and created the Age of Giants. These new found humanoids of large size overpowered the Dragons. This caused them to spawn the world as we know it, The New Age. Humanoids of all shapes and sizes were born. Harnessing the power of the Dragons and Giants they overcame all. Now they seek the find the king of the world. He who sits upon the Dragon Throne.
